Perfect Pre-Training Meal
Chicken Rice Bowl
High-carb · High-protein · Easy to digest
Servings
1
Ingredients
  • 100 gRice
  • 150 gChicken breast
  • 100 gBroccoli
  • ½Bell pepper
  • 1 tspOlive oil
  • —Salt & pepper
  • —Paprika / garlic powderoptional
Steps — tap to check off
  • 1
    Rinse rice and cook with 200 ml water + a pinch of salt.
  • 2
    Bring to a boil, then simmer 10–12 min. Let rest 5 min.
  • 3
    Cut broccoli into florets and slice the pepper.
  • 4
    Cut chicken into small pieces.
  • 5
    Heat oil in a pan, cook chicken 6–8 min. Season well.
  • 6
    Steam or sauté vegetables.
  • 7
    Serve everything in a bowl. 🎉
